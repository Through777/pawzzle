📖 Our Story

Why We Created Pawzzle

When my golden retriever, Maple, passed away last year, I found myself scrolling endlessly through my camera roll — desperate to hear her bark, see her tail wag, or watch her clumsy puppy zoomies one more time. But those videos were buried among thousands of files, impossible to find when grief hit hardest.

That's when the idea struck: what if I could hold a physical piece of her memory in my hands? A tangible object that, with a simple tap of my phone, could bring her back for a moment.

🧩✨📹🐾 Each puzzle tile = one precious memory, preserved forever

Pawzzle combines the comforting ritual of holding a physical memento with the magic of NFC technology. Each puzzle piece is custom-designed to reflect your pet's unique personality.

💡 The Pawzzle philosophy: Grief isn't something to "get over." It's something we learn to carry — and having a beautiful, tactile way to revisit joyful memories makes that weight a little lighter.

How It Works
